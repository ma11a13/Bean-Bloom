Mira’s tiny café serves coffee with a flower on every saucer. What began as a small gesture — a bloom beside a cup — has blossomed into a beloved local ritual. Here, rich espresso meets delicate petals, and every sip feels like a pause, a breath, a quiet joy.
People come for the coffee, but stay for the warmth. Each table holds a story, each cup a moment of calm. Whether you're meeting a friend, escaping the rush, or simply treating yourself, Mira’s offers more than caffeine — it offers care.
Lavender with your latte. Chamomile with your cappuccino. The flowers change with the seasons, but the feeling stays the same: welcoming, cozy, unforgettable.
